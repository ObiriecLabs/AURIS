## What is AURIS?

AURIS is a professional desktop application for audio and video transcription. It runs entirely on your machine — no cloud processing, no mandatory account, no file size limits.

Available in five tiers, from a one-time local Whisper license to the full Google Gemini AI suite (APEX) with OL-Managed keys.

| Tier | Price | Engine | Highlights |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Standard** | €29.99 one-time | Whisper local | Transcription + 6 output formats |
| **Pro** | €49.99 one-time | Whisper + AI (BYOK) | AI Intelligence + Audio Overview (ElevenLabs) |
| **Master** | €79.99 one-time | Pro + NLE + Video | NLE Console, animated subtitles, Video Overview (BYOK) |
| **Elite** | €39.99/month · 60 credits | Whisper + OL-Managed AI | Pro + Master features, all keys pre-configured (Anthropic, ElevenLabs, Runway, Mureka) |
| **APEX** | €149/month · 200 credits | Whisper + Full Gemini OL-Managed | Full Google Gemini suite — see below |

> **OL-Managed** — Obiriec Labs manages and pays all third-party AI API keys. You load AURIS credits and use everything. No separate accounts, no credit cards on other platforms.

## Transcription

### Local Whisper (all tiers)
- Runs 100% on your machine — no data leaves your computer
- No file size or duration limits
- 99 languages supported with auto-detect
- Output formats: `TXT` · `PDF` · `SRT` · `VTT` · `TSV` · `JSON`
- Real-time progress log, stop at any time

**Supported formats:** `MP3` · `MP4` · `WAV` · `M4A` · `FLAC` · `OGG` · `WEBM` · `MKV` · `AVI` · `MOV` · `OPUS` · `AAC`

| Model | Size | Recommended for |
|---|---|---|
| tiny | ~150 MB | Quick drafts, short content |
| base | ~290 MB | Light daily use |
| small | ~490 MB | Good speed/quality balance |
| **medium** | ~1.5 GB | **Recommended** — high quality |
| large | ~3.0 GB | Maximum precision |

### Gemini Transcription — APEX (2 credits)
- Cloud processing via Google Gemini 2.0 Flash
- No duration limit, automatic multilingual, superior quality on complex audio (accents, overlaps, noise)

## NLE Console (Master and above)

Integrated video editing console, opens in a dedicated fullscreen window.

- **3-panel layout:** Timeline (left) · Preview (center) · Inspector (right)
- Word-highlight animated subtitles (TikTok / Reels style)
- Multi-format export: 16:9 · 9:16 vertical · 1:1 square
- HyperFrames for motion graphics
- AI video generator integration
